What Is HELLO Trust?

Empowering Youth, Uplifting Lives, Inspiring Change

Welcome to Humanity’s Empowerment of Life & Leadership Opportunities (HELLO) Trust, a youth-led non-profit organisation dedicated to uplifting communities and empowering children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and Special Educational Needs (SEN) across Asia and beyond.

Founded by Casso Pi, HELLO Trust was created with a vision to provide meaningful leadership and service opportunities for students while delivering tangible, lasting impact to communities in need. The initiative began with the support of a music scholarship from Harrow International School Shenzhen and has since grown into a dynamic, student-led organisation operating across borders.

From its humble beginnings, HELLO Trust has evolved into an international platform committed to advancing social welfare, nurturing youth leadership, and supporting underprivileged and often overlooked individuals and communities. Today, our network of passionate student volunteers spans multiple cities and countries, united by a shared mission to create positive change.

At HELLO Trust, we believe in the power of collective action. Whether through fundraising initiatives, educational outreach, or hands-on community engagement, our work aims to foster compassion, collaboration, and long-term, meaningful impact.

Origin of HELLO Trust

Founder — Casso Pi

Founder — Casso Pi

Casso Pi is the Founder and current CEO of HELLO Trust. Once a quiet and reserved student himself, Casso understands how transformative the right opportunity can be. It was through the leadership roles offered to him in school that he discovered his voice, confidence, and purpose. Inspired by this journey, he founded HELLO Trust to ensure that other students — especially those who may lack access to such experiences — are given the platform they need to unlock their potential and make a difference. Under his leadership, HELLO Trust has grown from a local initiative in Shenzhen to a global network of passionate student volunteers committed to serving the underprivileged. As Casso continues his academic journey at the London School of Economics & Political Science (LSE), he remains deeply committed to expanding HELLO Trust’s reach and impact across the UK and Europe. Through HELLO Trust, Casso hopes to show that with the right guidance and opportunities, every young person can lead, serve, and create meaningful change.
